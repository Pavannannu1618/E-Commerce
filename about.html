Our Story

A shop born from
obsession with craft

It started in a small flat with a collection of ceramics too beautiful to keep to ourselves. We began photographing them, writing about them, sharing them — and quickly discovered we weren't alone in wanting objects that felt true and lasting.

AURA was founded in 2014 by two designers who believed that thoughtful commerce could coexist with genuine beauty. Not fast, not disposable — but considered, crafted, and cherished.

Today we work with 28 independent makers across 14 countries, each chosen for their commitment to materials, process, and honesty of form. Every object in our collection has a story, a person behind it, and a reason to exist.

Priya & Aryan Co-Founders, AURA
What We Stand For

Guided by three
principles

01
◈
Honest Craft

We only stock objects where the making matters. No hidden plastics, no deceptive finishes. Every piece is exactly what it appears to be — and that's something we take seriously.

02
⊕
Enduring Beauty

We ask one question about every piece: will this still feel right in ten years? If the answer is yes, it earns its place in our catalogue. Trend-chasing is the enemy of lasting beauty.

03
✦
Fair Partnership

Our makers are not suppliers — they're collaborators. We pay fairly, share honestly, and build long-term relationships that allow artisans to do their best work without compromise.

Our Journey

A decade of
quiet growth

2014
The first collection

AURA launches online with 12 ceramic pieces from 3 makers. Sells out in 48 hours. We knew we had something real.

2016
The first physical space

We open a small studio in Hyderabad — equal parts shop, workshop, and gathering place. A space where objects can be touched, felt, and understood.

2018
International makers join

Our network expands beyond India. We begin collaborating with artisans in Japan, Portugal, and Morocco — bringing new traditions into quiet conversation.

2021
The Edit launches

Our weekly newsletter becomes a destination in itself — 40,000 readers who trust our eye and come for the stories behind every object we choose.

2024
Ten years, and growing

We celebrate a decade with 840+ products, 28 maker partners, and a community that continues to teach us what it means to live beautifully.
